Open Projects

  • ACORN - Annotating Checkworthy Opinions with ReasoniNg

    Online misinformation spreads rapidly via social networks and deceptive websites posing as legitimate news sources. Identifying which information should be checked is the first step. In this project, you will link checkworthiness judgments to explicit human reasoning through human and machine annotation.
