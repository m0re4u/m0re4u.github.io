Global Game Jam 2020

During the 2020 Global Game Jam, my team and I worked on Re-Pair, a retro RTS in Pico-8. The goal of the game is to pair up units into bigger ones, and defeat the evil forces. Your territory grows by scouting with small units, so don’t pair them all!

You can play it below. Since it requires a mouse to be played, play it in your browser!
